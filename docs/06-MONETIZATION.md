# 06 — Business Model & Monetization

**Last Updated:** April 2026

---

## Business Model: Freemium + Subscription

LifeQuest uses a **freemium model** — free enough to get addicted, premium enough to justify $7.99/month.

The core loop (XP, quests, streak) is free forever. Revenue comes from users who want more: more skills, more AI interactions, advanced analytics, and cosmetics.

---

## Free Tier — "Explorer"

| Feature | Free |
|---|---|
| Active skills | Up to 2 |

| AI mentor messages/day | 5 |
| Quest history | 7 days |
| Achievements | 20 basic |

**Goal of free tier:** Create the habit loop. Make the user feel the value. Get them to the "I need more" moment.

## Monetization Strategies

### 1. Value-Trigger Conversion
Instead of "your 7-day trial expired", we trigger the upsell when the user hits a natural limit:
- "You've reached Level 10 in Piano! Upgrade to keep leveling up"
- "You've used all 5 AI messages today. Upgrade for unlimited"
- "Unlock 2 more skills with Pro"

This approach shows **280% higher conversion** than time-based trials.

### 2. Annual Plan Incentive
Annual plan at ~37% discount creates:
- Higher LTV per user
- Better cash flow for small team
- Lower churn (committed users)

### 3. Streak Freeze (Retention Tool)
Premium users get 2 streak freezes/month. This:
- Makes premium feel "protective" and valuable
- Reduces churn caused by missed days
- Creates urgency to stay premium

### 4. Rewarded Ads (Free Tier Only)
Free users can watch an optional rewarded ad to:
- Gain 1 extra AI message
- Get a streak freeze (1x per month)
- Unlock a bonus quest

This generates small ad revenue while giving free users a path to feel premium moments.

### 5. Future: Skill Packs (One-time purchase)
Curated quest packs for specific goals:
- "30-Day DJ Fundamentals" — R$29.90
- "Piano for Adults: Complete Beginner" — R$49.90
- "Build Your First App in 60 Days" — R$39.90

These are one-time purchases that generate revenue outside subscription.

## Cost Structure

| Cost | Monthly (at 1,000 users) |
|---|---|
| OpenAI API (avg user) | ~R$0.40/user = R$400 |
| Firebase (Firestore, Functions) | ~R$150 |
| RevenueCat | Free up to R$12,500 MRR |
| Apple Developer Program | R$550/year = ~R$46/month |
| **Total fixed + variable** | **~R$596/month** |

Break-even: ~15 premium subscribers at R$39.90/month

## App Store Pricing Strategy

- **Brazil (primary market):** R$39.90/month — accessible but aspirational
- **US/Global:** $7.99/month — competitive with Duolingo Plus ($6.99), Headspace ($12.99)
- **Annual:** 37% discount to maximize LTV
